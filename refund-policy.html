Refund Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

Thank you for choosing Bitzy. This Refund Policy explains how refunds are handled for subscriptions, premium features, and digital purchases.

Digital Products

Bitzy primarily provides digital content and services. Unless required by applicable law, purchases of digital products, subscriptions, and premium features are generally non-refundable once access has been granted.

Accidental Purchases

If you believe you made a purchase by mistake, please contact our support team as soon as possible. We will review requests on a case-by-case basis.

Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation prevents future billing but does not automatically result in a refund for the current billing period.

Google Play & Apple App Store Purchases

If your purchase was made through Google Play or the Apple App Store, refund requests are subject to the policies of the respective platform. Please use the refund process provided by that store.

Exceptional Circumstances

Bitzy may, at its sole discretion, issue a full or partial refund in situations such as duplicate charges, technical issues that prevent access to purchased services, or billing errors.

Contact Us

For refund-related questions, contact us at:

  • Email: support@bitzy.dev
  • Website: https://bitzy.dev

We aim to respond to refund inquiries within 3–5 business days.
