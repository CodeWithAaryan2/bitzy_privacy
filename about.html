About Bitzy

Level Up Your Coding Skills.

Who We Are

Bitzy is an interactive coding learning platform designed to help beginners and aspiring developers build real-world programming skills through structured courses, quizzes, coding challenges, achievements, XP, streaks, and certificates.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make high-quality programming education engaging, accessible, and enjoyable for learners around the world.

What We Offer

  • Interactive coding courses
  • Practice quizzes and coding challenges
  • Learning progress tracking
  • XP, achievements, and streaks
  • Certificates for completed courses
  • Responsive experience across supported devices

Our Values

  • Learning by doing
  • Continuous improvement
  • Accessibility and inclusivity
  • User privacy and security
  • Building a supportive learning community
