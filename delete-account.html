Delete Your Bitzy Account

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

At Bitzy, we respect your privacy and give you control over your personal data. If you would like to permanently delete your Bitzy account, you can request account deletion by following the instructions below.

Important:
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, your learning progress, achievements, XP, streaks, and associated personal information may no longer be recoverable.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@bitzy.dev.
  2. Use the same email address that is registered with your Bitzy account.
  3. Use the subject line "Delete My Bitzy Account".
  4. Our support team will verify your request.
  5. Your account deletion request will normally be processed within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your Bitzy account.
  • Your profile information.
  • Your learning progress.
  • Your XP, achievements and streaks.
  • Your certificates (where applicable).
  • Your community profile information.

What Data May Be Retained?

Certain information may be retained for a limited period where required by applicable law, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or to enforce our legal obligations.
